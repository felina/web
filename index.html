Felina

Felina is a system for analysing images of wildlife

Felina connects wildlife photographers and enthusiasts with academics and researchers. Upload your images today to contribute to conservation efforts and learn more about your favourite animals.

It breaks down the barriers between academics and the public and allows both group to benefit from the other's work.

For wildlife lovers

Upload your images and videos of wildlife. Add information and tags. Help conservation research, and get access to statistics and news.

For researchers

Get access to the worlds largest database of wildlife media and metadata to use in your research. Contribute the results of your studies straight back to the people who made them possible.
