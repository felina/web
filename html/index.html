Felina is a system for analysing images of wildlife

Felina connects wildlife photographers and enthusiasts with academics and researchers. Upload your images today to contribute to conservation efforts and learn more about your favourite animals.

It breaks down the barriers between academics and the public and allows both groups to benefit from the other's work.

For enthusiasts

Whether you're a hobbyist photographer, an eco-tourist or just a casual zoo visitor, we want your photos. Upload your images and videos of wildlife and add information and tags and you'll help conservation research, and get access to statistics and news.

For researchers

Get access to the worlds largest database of wildlife media and metadata to use in your research. Computer vision specialists get a huge data set to work with, and biologists and conservationists get valuable information to further their cause. Then, contribute the results of your studies straight back to the people who made them possible.

For Nature

Help to conserve the species you love for future generations to enjoy, protecting endangered species and fostering rich biodiversity.
